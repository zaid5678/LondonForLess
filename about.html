The Story

How It
Started

London For Less started with a simple, slightly frustrated observation: this city is extraordinary, but it often feels like it's only accessible if you've got money to burn. Tickets to exhibitions, entry fees, minimum spends, overpriced everything. It didn't add up.

So we started keeping notes — every freebie spotted, every pop-up discovered, every deal that made us do a double-take. We shared them online. A few friends followed. Then their friends. Then, somewhere along the way, things got properly mad.

Today, London For Less reaches over 10 million people every single month. We've been featured on the BBC, shared by celebrities, and cited as the resource that changed how Londoners think about experiencing their own city.

The mission hasn't changed since day one: find the best free events, the most ridiculous deals, the pop-ups worth queuing for, and the hidden gems that most people walk straight past. Verify everything before we post it. Make it easy to act on. Then share it with as many people as possible.

Because London should be for everyone — the student, the young professional, the family trying to do something brilliant on a tight budget, the tourist who wants the real experience. Not just those who can afford the ticket price.
